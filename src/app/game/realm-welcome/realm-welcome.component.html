This is the welcome page of the realm. You can see all the other players. The realm owner can edit the realm description and it willl be visible for all realm users.

How to use the left menu?

view_moduleModules
Modules are the way we group everything else. Characters, sheets, maps, images... everything belongs to a module. This should be your first thing to visit when you create a new realm.

layersDefinition
This is the page where you can describe the characters in your game. You tell us all the properties an entity has (name, attributes, gear) and later on your players will be able to use them.

crop_pasteTemplates
Templates are the way to create lot of characters easily. You provide the default values and we will create entities using them.

person_pinEntities
These are the characters in the game. The game master creates them from definitions or templates, and the players can use them.

library_booksSheet
The built-in entity viewer gets the job done but every game deserves a nice character sheet. Use you html and css skills to create your own.

content_copyTables
Checking rules, calculating treasures and xp. You can store everything you need in tables. The best part? You can use tables to calculate modifiers, bonuses seemlessly!

imageImages
Player tokens, a nice looking chair, stone background for the caves? You store them here. You can use our image finder or upload your own.

mapMaps
You will create different maps for your encounters and dungeons. You will be able to send the players to those maps.

tabBoard
This is where you play. Navigate to this page when you are ready to investigate the maps ceated by the dungeon master.
